Add a Purchase section inside the Accounts module to manage Purchase Orders, Vendor Bills, and Other Expenses.

Follow structure below.

---

MODULE NAME

Accounts Purchase Module

---

SECTION 1 — SIDEBAR NAVIGATION

Add menu:

Accounts → Purchases

Sub-sections:

Purchase Orders

Vendor Bills

Other Expenses

---

SECTION 2 — PURCHASE ORDERS VIEW (FINANCIAL SUMMARY ONLY)

Display list from:

purchase_orders table

Columns:

PO Number

Vendor Name

Reference (Swatch / Style / Manual)

PO Amount

Received Quantity

Pending Quantity

PO Status

PO Date

Allow:

View PO Details

Download PO PDF

Navigate to linked PR records

---

SECTION 3 — VENDOR BILLS VIEW

Display vendor bills created from:

Purchase Receipt vendor invoice upload

Manual vendor bills

Columns:

Bill Number

Vendor Name

Linked PO

Bill Amount

Paid Amount

Pending Amount

Bill Status

Bill Date

Allow:

Open bill details

Record payment

View attachment

---

SECTION 4 — OTHER EXPENSES ENTRY

Create table:

other_expenses

Fields:

expense_id

expense_number (auto generated)

expense_category

vendor_id (optional)

reference_type (optional)

reference_id (optional)

amount

currency_code

payment_status

payment_type

expense_date

remarks

attachment

created_by

created_at

---

SECTION 5 — EXPENSE CATEGORY VALUES

expense_category examples:

Courier Charges

Office Expenses

Sampling Misc Expenses

Transport Charges

Packaging Expenses

Utility Expenses

Other

Allow adding custom categories.

---

SECTION 6 — VENDOR LEDGER INTEGRATION

When:

Vendor Bill created

OR

Other Expense linked to vendor created

Create entry inside:

vendor_ledger

Fields mapped:

vendor_id

reference_type

reference_id

entry_type = Expense

amount

expense_date

status = payment_status

---

SECTION 7 — PAYMENT RECORD SUPPORT

Allow recording payment against:

Vendor Bills

Other Expenses

Fields:

payment_type

payment_amount

payment_date

transaction_reference

status

Update:

vendor outstanding summary automatically

---

SECTION 8 — FILTERS

Allow filters on:

Vendor

Date Range

Expense Category

Status

Reference Type

---

SECTION 9 — SUCCESS MESSAGE

After saving expense:

Display:

Expense recorded successfully and added to vendor ledger

END OF MODULE
