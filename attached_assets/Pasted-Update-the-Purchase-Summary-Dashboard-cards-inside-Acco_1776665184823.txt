Update the Purchase Summary Dashboard cards inside:

Accounts → Purchases Summary

to include Purchase Orders and Purchase Receipts data in totals.

Follow structure below.

---

MODULE NAME

Enhanced Purchase Summary Cards Engine

---

SECTION 1 — PURCHASE SUMMARY CARD STRUCTURE

Replace existing purchase cards with:

Total Purchase Orders

Total Purchase Receipts

Total Vendor Bills

Total Paid to Vendors

Pending Vendor Payables

Other Expenses Total

---

SECTION 2 — TOTAL PURCHASE ORDERS CARD

Fetch from:

purchase_orders table

Display:

Total PO Amount

Pending PO Amount

Completed PO Amount

Allow button:

View Purchase Orders

Redirect to:

Purchase Orders module

---

SECTION 3 — TOTAL PURCHASE RECEIPTS CARD

Fetch from:

purchase_receipts table

Display:

Total PR Amount

Received Quantity Value

Pending Delivery Value

Allow button:

View Purchase Receipts

Redirect to:

Purchase Receipts module

---

SECTION 4 — TOTAL VENDOR BILLS CARD

Fetch from:

vendor bills linked with PR

Display:

Total Vendor Bill Amount

Paid Amount

Pending Amount

Redirect button:

View Vendor Bills

---

SECTION 5 — TOTAL PAID TO VENDORS CARD

Fetch from:

vendor_ledger

Include entry types:

Vendor Payment

Artwork Vendor Payment

Costing Vendor Payment

Other Expense Payment

Display:

Total Paid Amount

---

SECTION 6 — PENDING VENDOR PAYABLES CARD

Fetch from:

vendor_ledger

Include:

PR vendor invoices

costing outsource payments

artwork outsource payments

other expenses

Display:

Total Pending Payables

---

SECTION 7 — OTHER EXPENSES SUMMARY CARD

Fetch from:

other_expenses table

Display:

Total Expense Amount

Paid Expense Amount

Pending Expense Amount

Redirect button:

View Other Expenses

---

SECTION 8 — FILTER SUPPORT

All purchase summary cards must respect:

Date Range filter

Vendor filter

Expense Category filter

---

SECTION 9 — UI STRUCTURE

Display cards in grid layout:

Row 1:

Purchase Orders

Purchase Receipts

Vendor Bills

Row 2:

Paid to Vendors

Pending Vendor Payables

Other Expenses

Use:

dashboard counter card styling

consistent spacing

consistent icon placement

---

SECTION 10 — SUCCESS MESSAGE

After dashboard loads:

Display:

Enhanced purchase summary loaded successfully

END OF MODULE
