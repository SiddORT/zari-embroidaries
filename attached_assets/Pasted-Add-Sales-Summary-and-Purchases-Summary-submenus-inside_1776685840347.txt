Add Sales Summary and Purchases Summary submenus inside the Accounts module.

These pages must show financial overview only using existing tables.

Do NOT create duplicate transaction tables.

Follow structure below.

---

MODULE NAME

Accounts Sales and Purchases Summary Pages

---

SECTION 1 — SIDEBAR NAVIGATION

Inside:

Accounts

Add submenus:

Sales Summary

Purchases Summary

---

SECTION 2 — GLOBAL FILTER ROW

Add filters at top of both pages:

Date Range

Client selector (Sales Summary only)

Vendor selector (Purchases Summary only)

Filters must refresh data instantly.

---

SECTION 3 — SALES SUMMARY PAGE

Create page:

Accounts → Sales Summary

Display summary cards:

Total Invoiced Amount

Total Received Amount

Pending Receivables

Overdue Receivables

Fetch data from:

invoices

invoice_payments

---

SECTION 4 — SALES BREAKDOWN PANEL

Display additional cards:

Total Credit Notes Issued

Shipping Amount Recovered

Net Sales Value

Formula:

Net Sales Value =

Total Invoiced − Credit Notes

---

SECTION 5 — PURCHASE SUMMARY PAGE

Create page:

Accounts → Purchases Summary

Display summary cards:

Total Purchase Orders

Total Vendor Bills

Total Paid to Vendors

Pending Vendor Payables

Fetch data from:

purchase_orders

purchase_receipts

vendor_ledger

---

SECTION 6 — PURCHASE EXPENSE PANEL

Display additional card:

Other Expenses Total

Fetch from:

other_expenses table

---

SECTION 7 — REVENUE SNAPSHOT CARD

Display card on both pages:

Net Revenue

Formula:

Net Revenue =

Total Sales − Total Purchases

Highlight visually.

---

SECTION 8 — LIVE DATA REQUIREMENT

Both pages must:

fetch real-time values

respect filters

refresh after filter change

not cache totals

---

SECTION 9 — UI STRUCTURE

Layout order:

Filter Row

Summary Cards

Breakdown Cards

Revenue Snapshot Card

Use existing dashboard counter-card styling.

---

SECTION 10 — PERMISSIONS CONTROL

Allow access:

Accounts users

Admin users

Restrict:

non-accounts users

---

SECTION 11 — SUCCESS MESSAGE

After page loads:

Display:

Financial summary loaded successfully

END OF MODULE
