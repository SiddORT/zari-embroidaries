Build the Invoice Master Engine for an embroidery sampling ERP system.

This module must support:

Client invoices

Vendor bills

Multi-currency support

Exchange-rate snapshot locking

Multiple invoices per order

Invoice type classification

Follow structure exactly as defined below.

---

MODULE NAME

Invoice Master Engine

---

SECTION 1 — INVOICE HEADER TABLE

Create table:

invoices

Fields:

invoice_id (primary key)

invoice_number (auto generated)

invoice_direction

invoice_type

client_id

vendor_id

reference_type

reference_id

currency_code

exchange_rate_snapshot

invoice_currency_amount

base_currency_amount

subtotal_amount

shipping_amount

adjustment_amount

total_amount

received_amount

pending_amount

invoice_status

invoice_date

due_date

remarks

created_by

created_at

updated_at

---

SECTION 2 — INVOICE NUMBER FORMAT

Format:

INV-YYYY-00001

Example:

INV-2026-00021

Invoice number must be:

unique

sequential

non-editable after creation

---

SECTION 3 — INVOICE DIRECTION SUPPORT

invoice_direction values:

Client

Vendor

Validation rules:

If invoice_direction = Client

client_id required

vendor_id must be NULL

If invoice_direction = Vendor

vendor_id required

client_id must be NULL

---

SECTION 4 — INVOICE TYPE CLASSIFICATION ENGINE

Add field:

invoice_type

Supported values:

Proforma

Advance

Partial

Material Recovery

Artwork Charges

Courier Charges

Final Invoice

Custom

System must allow:

multiple invoices per reference_id

Example:

reference_type = Swatch

reference_id = SW1023

must support:

multiple invoice records

Do NOT restrict invoice creation to one per order.

---

SECTION 5 — REFERENCE LINKING ENGINE

reference_type values:

Swatch

Style

Quotation

Purchase Receipt

Shipping

Artwork

Manual

reference_id stores related record ID.

---

SECTION 6 — MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT

Store inside invoices table:

currency_code

exchange_rate_snapshot

invoice_currency_amount

base_currency_amount

Formula:

base_currency_amount =
invoice_currency_amount × exchange_rate_snapshot

Exchange rate must remain locked after save.

Do NOT dynamically recalculate later.

---

SECTION 7 — PAYMENT TRACKING PREPARATION

Store:

received_amount

pending_amount

Calculation:

pending_amount =
total_amount − received_amount

Default values:

received_amount = 0

pending_amount = total_amount

---

SECTION 8 — SHIPPING COST SUPPORT

Allow storing:

shipping_amount

Shipping amount may come from:

order_shipping_details table

Allow manual override.

---

SECTION 9 — ADJUSTMENT AMOUNT SUPPORT

Allow storing:

adjustment_amount

Used for:

round-off

manual correction

credit adjustment

Allow negative values.

---

SECTION 10 — SUCCESS MESSAGE

After saving invoice:

Display:

Invoice saved successfully

END OF PROMPT 1
