Build the Invoice Payment Engine for the embroidery sampling ERP system.

This module must support:

Client invoice payment tracking

Vendor bill payment tracking

Partial payments

Multiple payments per invoice

Multi-currency payment recording

Exchange-rate snapshot locking

Automatic invoice status updates

Client ledger integration

Vendor ledger integration

Follow structure exactly as defined below.

---

MODULE NAME

Invoice Payment Engine

---

SECTION 1 — PAYMENT TABLE STRUCTURE

---

Create table:

invoice_payments

Fields:

payment_id (primary key)

invoice_id

payment_direction

party_id

payment_type

payment_amount

currency_code

exchange_rate_snapshot

base_currency_amount

transaction_reference

payment_status

payment_date

remarks

attachment

created_by

created_at

updated_at

payment_direction values:

Received

Paid

Logic:

Client Invoice → Received

Vendor Bill → Paid

---

SECTION 2 — PAYMENT TYPE VALUES

---

payment_type values:

Cash

Bank Transfer

UPI

Cheque

Online Gateway

Adjustment

Other

---

SECTION 3 — PAYMENT STATUS VALUES

---

payment_status values:

Processing

Completed

Failed

---

SECTION 4 — MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT

---

Allow payments in:

invoice currency

OR

base currency

Store:

currency_code

exchange_rate_snapshot

Calculate:

base_currency_amount

Formula:

base_currency_amount =
payment_amount × exchange_rate_snapshot

Lock exchange_rate_snapshot after save.

---

SECTION 5 — MULTIPLE PAYMENTS PER INVOICE SUPPORT

---

Allow:

multiple payment entries

against same invoice

Example:

Invoice amount = 10,000

Payment 1 = 4,000

Payment 2 = 3,000

Payment 3 = 3,000

Final status:

Paid

---

SECTION 6 — AUTOMATIC INVOICE STATUS UPDATE

---

After each payment entry:

Update:

received_amount

pending_amount

Apply rules:

If pending_amount = total_amount

status = Generated

If pending_amount > 0 AND pending_amount < total_amount

status = Partially Paid

If pending_amount = 0

status = Paid

---

SECTION 7 — CLIENT LEDGER ENTRY CREATION

---

If payment_direction = Received

Create ledger entry inside:

Client Ledger

Fields mapped:

client_id

invoice_id

entry_type = Payment Received

payment_amount

payment_date

transaction_reference

status = payment_status

---

SECTION 8 — VENDOR LEDGER ENTRY CREATION

---

If payment_direction = Paid

Create ledger entry inside:

Vendor Ledger

Fields mapped:

vendor_id

invoice_id

entry_type = Vendor Payment

payment_amount

payment_date

transaction_reference

status = payment_status

---
